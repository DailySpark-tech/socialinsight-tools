About Our Facebook Tools

SocialInsight Tools offers estimation-based Facebook tools created to help users understand engagement and interaction patterns on Facebook without accessing private data or requiring logins. All results are for educational and informational purposes only.

These tools do not use the official Facebook API and are not affiliated with Meta or Facebook in any way. Instead, they use general, non-private signals to estimate interaction trends and behavior patterns.

Whether you want to explore general engagement estimation or interaction insights, our Facebook tool collection provides a privacy-safe and straightforward experience.
