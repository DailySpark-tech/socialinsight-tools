Frequently Asked Questions

Can I see who viewed my Instagram profile?

No. Instagram does not allow profile view tracking. Our tools provide estimation-based interaction analysis only.

Is SocialInsight Tools affiliated with Instagram or Meta?

No. SocialInsight Tools is an independent platform and has no affiliation with Instagram, Meta, WhatsApp, or Facebook.

Do I need to log in or provide my password?

No. We never request passwords, logins, or private account access.

Are the results accurate?

Results are estimation-based and may not be accurate. They are intended for informational and educational purposes only.

Is it safe to use SocialInsight Tools?

Yes. We do not connect to third-party APIs or access private data.
